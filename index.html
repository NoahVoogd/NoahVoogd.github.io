TOPforms
During my time working as a front-end developer at Smart Form Solutions, I redesigned and rewrote the TOPforms app as a cross-platform application for Android and iOS. Topforms allows user to fill out forms on a mobile device and send the forms directly to their company's back office. The vision behind the app is to stay true to the layout and feel of the original paper form in order to make life easier for the user, while still facilitating the switch from paper to digital.

The app can be found on Google Play and the App Store.
Smart Form Solutions was purchased by OutSmart in late 2020, who are currently working on incorporating TOPforms into their product line.
Made with
HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Cordova, SQL
Parsafix
While interning at Canon Production Printing I designed and developed an open-source tool that makes real-time collaboration possible between two different model engineering applications. The tool is called Parsafix and was written as a Node.js application. It can parse and communicate domain-specific models between different types of language workbench clients.
That's a lot of jargon and it may not sound super flashy, but Parsafix has a lot of potential when it comes to making system modeling more collaborative. A paper describing the tool was submitted to the HoWCoM conference 2021. Canon will be using the tool in their efforts to optimize their system design processes.

More information as well as the tool's implementation can be found here.
Made with
JavaScript, Node.js, JetBrains MPS, HTML/CSS

Jam
As one of many side-projects, I've been working on a mobile game called Jam. It's far from finished, but I'm quite happy with the overall aesthetic. The game is split up into three different parts, each presenting a different type of puzzle. The goal is to go from sorting fruit, to squashing the fruit into jam, to loading the jam into a delivery truck.
I decided to develop the game using Godot, a free open-source game engine. I work on Jam every now and then when I have some free time and so far it's been a lot of fun to develop.
Made with
Godot, GDScript, Photoshop
